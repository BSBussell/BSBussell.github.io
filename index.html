About The Bee

I am a lot of things, and it’s kinda crazy. I grew up making calculator games for my friends in middle school, which slowly evolved into iPad games, and random encryption apps for my friends to share secret messages.

I got a degree in Computer Science from the University of Tennessee, and since then, I have continued building things using the skills I learned from that experience.

I aim to create things that can be enjoyed by people who want to be changed. I want to create games that change people and make them see the world differently, even if it’s in the smallest of ways. For me, media of all kinds have always impacted and changed me. My goal is to create something that impacts someone else the way the stores I grew up with impacted me.
